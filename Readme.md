Journal-Guinevere (GUIN AI)


Journal-Guinevere isn't just another project; it’s my personal engine for growth. I’m building GUIN AI to serve as an intelligent, reflective companion—one that helps me parse my daily thoughts, optimize my productivity, and stay disciplined as I prepare for the road ahead.


I am building this system to bridge the gap between human intent and automated execution. GUIN AI is designed to act as a mirror and a catalyst, turning my daily journals into actionable insights while keeping my academic and career goals in sharp focus. This is where I experiment with AI, engineering, and the discipline required to build something that lasts.



I’m keeping the architecture modular and professional:

Frontend: React.js paired with Tailwind CSS. I want the interface to be clean, intuitive, and distraction-free.

Backend: Python. This is the core logic where I build the "brain" of the project—handling data flow, memory, and AI integration.

Structure: I’m maintaining a clean separation of concerns, ensuring that my logic is decoupled from the UI for better scalability.
